Do you own a special collection? Coins, stamps, action figures, holiday ornaments, books, videos, designer purses, even Beanie Babies. Are you prepared with documentation in the event that your collection is destroyed or stolen? My Collections app provides a central repository for photos, copies of receipts, and information about every item in your collection. You can use this app to catalog all your collections. It's easy to enter data for new acquisitions and to compile a wish list. With the click of a button, you can share photos of an item with a fellow collector. Or you can shop for items on your wish list.
